Kazakhstan at the moment

It all started with the Kazakh Khanate - from those times when our ancestors, fighting for freedom, honor and unity, created the basis for the future. Their heroism and perseverance, bloody wars and courage became the foundation of the independence of our country. Today, every citizen of Kazakhstan is grateful to these great sons and daughters for the opportunity to live in peace and harmony. Their exploits will forever remain in our hearts, inspiring us to carry on their legacy.
